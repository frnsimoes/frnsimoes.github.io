### About this website

This blog is a collection of my notes and casual experiments, but it also serves as an incentive for me to keep writing about the things I love and to procrastinate in a [structured way]. 

Besides that, to paraphrase Julia Evans, who is an inspiration for this webpage, "this blog is about how being clear & curious & humble is better than sounding like I know it all already".
