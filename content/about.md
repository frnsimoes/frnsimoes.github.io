### About this website

This blog is a collection of my notes and casual experiments. It also serves as an incentive for me to keep writing about the things I love and to procrastinate in a [structured way].

### About me

I grew up in the 90s, fascinated by this magical thing called a computer. I'm currently a SWE, but what I truly aspire to be is just *the computer guy*, wandering between shell sessions and syscalls.

Literature is another passion of mine. I even wrote a [full comparison of Shakespare's editions and their translations to Portuguese](https://fsimoesgarcia.medium.com/a-pregui%C3%A7osos-e-diletantes-shakespeare-51d18ca52377).
