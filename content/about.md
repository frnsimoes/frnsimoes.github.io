### About the blog

This blog is a collection of my notes and casual experiments. It also serves as an incentive for me to keep writing about the things I love. 

### About me

I'm a software developer. Currently, I live in Brazil with my wife and two kids. I grew up in the '90s, so I have a natural love for computers and the internet as a "promise of a better world." My interest in systems is nothing more than a reflection of this sentiment. 

Since the beginning of 2024, I've been dedicating a lot of time to know more about computers by studying operating systems, networks, databases, etc. This has profoundly changed the way I approach my job, and I’m having as much fun as I did when I first learned how to program. (Or, you may say, as much fun as I did when I was a child).

Before becoming a developer, I read a bunch of classical literature and philosophy. I worked as a lawyer, copyeditor, proofreader (portuguese), and learning assistant. f you are curious, I wrote a brief [summary] of the past years.

If you want to nerd out and talk about these topics, feel free to send me an [email] or a message on [Linkedin].
